Metric: PSNR (GT)
Value: Higher = closer pixel reconstruction to ground truth (dB).
Why: Fast sanity check for pixel-level accuracy.

Metric: SSIM (GT)
Value: Higher = better structural similarity (edges/contrast/structure) to ground truth, usually [0,1].
Why: Captures structural correctness better than raw pixel metrics.

Metric: LPIPS (GT)
Value: Lower = more perceptually similar to ground truth (deep feature distance).
Why: Best “looks similar” metric; correlates better with human judgment.

Metric: Boundary PSNR (GT)
Value: Higher = pixel fidelity, computed only in a narrow border band around mask edge.
Why: Directly measures seam errors near the mask boundary.

Metric: Boundary SSIM (GT)
Value: Higher = structural continuity near the mask edge (border band only).
Why: Evaluates structure alignment at the seam (where artifacts are most visible).

Metric: Boundary LPIPS (GT)
Value: Lower = perceptual seam quality near the boundary (border band only).
Why: Strong indicator of visible seam artifacts.

Metric: Boundary Gradient L1 (GT)
Value: Lower = closer edge/gradient behavior to GT near the boundary.
Why: Detects halos, ringing, blur, and edge discontinuities.

Metric: Prompt-Image CLIP Similarity (No-GT)
Value: Higher = prompt and image are more semantically aligned (cosine similarity in CLIP space).
Why: Measures whether your inpaint content matches the prompt meaning.

Metric: NIQE (No-GT)
Value: Lower = more “natural” looking image according to natural image statistics.
Why: Detects synthetic artifacts without any ground truth.

Metric: BRISQUE (No-GT)
Value: Lower = fewer distortions / artifacts (blind quality assessment).
Why: Another no-reference artifact detector; complements NIQE.

Metric: ImageReward (No-GT)
Value: Higher = better quality/alignment per a learned human preference model.
Why: Often correlates with human preference for text-guided images.

Metric: Border Leakage MAE (No-GT)
Value: Lower = output matches the original on the KEEP side near the border (outside-mask band).
Why: Detects “bleeding” / unintended changes outside the mask.

Metric: Border Gradient Jump (No-GT)
Value: Lower = smoother gradient continuity across the seam (difference between inside-band and outside-band gradients).
Why: Measures seam harshness even without GT.
